How to Use

  1. Prepare your website file (must be .html or .htm).
  2. Click the Open Web Publisher Tool button above.
  3. Click the upload area and select your file.
  4. Wait for the progress bar to complete.
  5. Once finished, copy your unique public link or open it directly.

Note: Your file history is saved automatically to your device for easy access later.

Privacy Policy

1. Local Storage Data
To ensure your privacy, the list of your uploaded websites ("History") is stored strictly in your browser's Local Storage. This means user A cannot see the files uploaded by user B. Your dashboard is private to your specific device.

2. Hosting Mechanism
Files are securely uploaded to our repository. While the generated link is public (so you can share it), the management of these files is restricted to the administrator and your local device history.

3. Data Collection
We do not collect personal data, cookies, or track your browsing behavior. This tool is a direct utility for developers.
